## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security vulnerabilities by emailing security@astraos-example.com instead of opening a public issue. We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 48 hours.

We will try to provide a timeline for fixing the issue and ask for your permission before publishing any reports.
